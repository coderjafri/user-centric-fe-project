Wasim Akram - Fast Bowling Coach

Nicknamed "King of Swing", Wasim Akram needs no introduction as he is arguably the best left arm fast bowler to have ever played the game of cricket. He was known to be a master a swinging the ball in both directions and especially dangerous when the ball started to reverse.

Vivian Richards - Batting Coach

Nicknamed "Master Blaster", Vivian Richards was probably the most feared batsman of all time as he was an aggressive batsman who would take on any bowler in the world. Imran Khan has said that Vivian Richards presence at the crease would intimidate him.

Shane Warne - Spin Coach

First known for the "Gatting ball" where he bowled one of the best deliveries a batsman has ever faced. The best spinner to have ever played cricket, period. A massive spinner of the cricket ball, well known for his cricket brain and how he would set batsman up for failure.
